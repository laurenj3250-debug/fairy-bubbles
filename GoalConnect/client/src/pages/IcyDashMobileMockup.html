Design Decisions

  • 1. Single column - All widgets stack vertically for easy thumb scrolling
  • 2. 4 focused widgets - Weekly Goals, Habits, Monthly Progress, Schedule
  • 3. 3-day schedule - Yesterday/Today/Tomorrow with arrow navigation
  • 4. Bottom nav - Thumb-friendly navigation replaces sidebar
  • 5. Touch targets - Large +/checkbox buttons (44px+ tap area)
  • 6. Compact header - Logo + key stats, no wasted space
